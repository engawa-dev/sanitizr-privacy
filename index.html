Privacy Policy for Sanitizr

Effective Date: June 20, 2026

Sanitizr ("the Application") is a desktop utility developed by engawa-dev.

Summary

Sanitizr runs entirely on your device. It does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, usage data, or the contents of the files you process. There is no telemetry and no analytics, and the Application does not make network connections to send your data anywhere.

How the Application Works

Sanitizr masks sensitive information — such as email addresses, phone numbers, payment card numbers, IP addresses, URLs, and any custom words you specify — in documents that you choose. All processing is performed locally and offline:

  • The files you select are read and processed only on your device.
  • The masked output files and the “un-mask” key files are written to folders on your own device. They are never uploaded or sent to us or to any third party.
  • The un-mask key files stay on your device and remain under your control. Keep them private — anyone with a key file can reverse the masking.

Information We Collect

None. We do not collect, receive, or have access to your files, the values that are masked, your key files, or any usage information.

If you obtain the Application or any paid features through the Microsoft Store, your purchase, licensing, and any rating or review you choose to submit are handled by the Microsoft Store and are subject to Microsoft’s Privacy Statement. We do not receive your payment details.

Children’s Privacy

The Application is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone.
