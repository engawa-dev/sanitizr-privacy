# Privacy Policy for Sanitizr

**Effective Date: June 27, 2026**

Sanitizr ("the Application") is a desktop utility developed by engawa-dev.

The rendered version of this policy is published at
**https://engawa-dev.github.io/sanitizr-privacy/**

## Summary

Sanitizr processes your documents locally on your device. It does **not** collect, store,
transmit, or share the contents of the files you process, the values that are masked, or
your un-mask key files. There is no telemetry and no analytics.

If you use a paid Pro license, the Application may contact the licensing provider only to
activate, validate, or deactivate your license. That licensing request does not include the
contents of your documents.

## How the Application Works

Sanitizr masks sensitive information — such as email addresses, phone numbers, payment card
numbers, IP addresses, URLs, and any custom words you specify — in documents that you
choose. Document processing is performed locally:

- The files you select are read and processed only on your device.
- The masked output files and the "un-mask" key files are written to folders on your own
  device. They are never uploaded or sent to us or to any third party.
- The un-mask key files stay on your device and remain under your control. Keep them
  private — anyone with a key file can reverse the masking.

## Information We Collect

We do not collect, receive, or have access to your files, the values that are masked, your
un-mask key files, or any usage information.

## Licensing and Purchases

For the Mac version, paid licenses are handled by Lemon Squeezy. When you activate,
validate, or deactivate a Pro license, the Application sends your license key and a computer
name to Lemon Squeezy so the license can be managed. Sanitizr does not send your documents,
masked values, un-mask key files, or custom word lists as part of license activation.

Purchases and payment processing are handled by Lemon Squeezy and are subject to
[Lemon Squeezy's Privacy Policy](https://www.lemonsqueezy.com/privacy). We do not receive
your payment card details.

## Microsoft Store

If you obtain the Windows version of the Application or any paid features through the
Microsoft Store, your
purchase, licensing, and any rating or review you choose to submit are handled by the
Microsoft Store and are subject to
[Microsoft's Privacy Statement](https://privacy.microsoft.com/privacystatement). We do not
receive your payment details.

## Children's Privacy

The Application is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect any information
from anyone.
